How Jobsdb uses your data

Using data based on employers' past behaviour, your default resume and other factors, Jobsdb predicts when you're likely to be a strong candidate for a role and may display this information to you and employers.

If you do not have a default resume, the resumé you use in an application becomes your default resume. If your visibility setting is set to Standard, employers searching for candidates outside of a job ad can view your Jobsdb Profile and your default resume.

In an application, we remove external links from documents to adhere to our security standards.

If you delete a file from your profile after sending it in an application, employers will still have access to it on the Jobsdb platform for a period of time.

If selected by the hirer for an on-platform reference check, you may be contacted by email, SMS or instant messaging services by SEEK Pass (a wholly owned Jobsdb business).
